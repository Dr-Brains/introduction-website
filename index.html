keyboards

As a developer (or a designer), you spend your whole day behind a computer, giving commands to the machine via your mouse and keyboard. Your keyboard is an extension of yourself. So why not do something nice with your input device?

This is where mechanical keyboards come in to play. There are literally endless possibilities when it comes to customising mechanical keyboards: form factor, case, layout, type of switches, keycaps, cable, ... Everything can be adjusted to suit your taste.

My taste happens to be the 'Planck' keyboard. A tiny, ortholinear (that means the keys are in a grid, not staggered) keyboard with 100% customizability through the firmware.

There is a lot more to say about keyboards, so if you're interested, ask about them in class ;)
